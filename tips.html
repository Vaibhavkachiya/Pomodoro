🔥 Productivity Tips

  • 💡 Take a 5-minute break after every 25 minutes of focused work.
  • 🎯 Set clear and achievable goals before starting a work session.
  • ☕ Stay hydrated and avoid too much caffeine to maintain focus.
  • 📵 Turn off notifications and distractions while working.
  • 🧘‍♂️ Stretch and relax during breaks to avoid burnout.
  • 📋 Prioritize tasks and break large projects into smaller steps.
  • 🎶 Listen to background music to improve concentration.
  • 🕒 Maintain a work-life balance by setting limits on work time.
